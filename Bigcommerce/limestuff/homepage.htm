Welcome to Lime stuff LTD.

Based in Whiteparish, near Salisbury in Wiltshire we sell a range of lime products.

Our products include matured lime putty, pre-blended lime putty mortars and plasters, natural hydraulic limes, cob bricks, tools and accessories.

Our customers range from homeowners to large plastering and building contractors. We provide a comprehensive support and advisory service whether you require one tub of lime putty or an order of lime mortar delivered in dumpy bags. We hold a range of stock which can be bought from our barn on site or we can deliver nationwide.
