Privacy Policy

Baby Beanbags is fully committed to providing a secure website and shopping environment and to protecting your privacy. In accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 and the December 2001 amendments to the ‘Act’, any personal information collected through Babybeanbags.com.au will be handled with the strictest confidence; stored securely and not on-sold or shared with any third party.

Personal Information
Baby Beanbags collects personal information at various points of the website navigation. The information collected through this website remains the sole property of Baby Beanbags and will not be sold to or shared with any other organization or third party. Information such as email, and name details will be gathered through your membership registration. When you register to shop, any personal information provided, such as email and delivery address, phone numbers, birth dates etc, will remain strictly confidential.

Credit Card Information
All monetary transactions are carried out within the trusted third party online payment environment of Paypal. No credit card numbers are stored onsite. All transactions and credit card information are handled by PayPal only. Credit card transactions are processed by a secure payment gateway, which adheres to the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DSS). This eliminates the risk of your credit card details being captured and used by unauthorised people. Each order is checked, one by one by our accounts department before processing. This reduces the possibility of credit card fraud. Ecommerce functionality programmed and maintained by eWave. A sophisticated Australian website developer specialising in internet security and watertight website infrastructure. This means that when you order a product from our website, you can rest assured that the highest security checks have been put in place to make sure that your details will not fall into the wrong hands.

Automatically Logged InformationDue to the nature of the internet, there is certain information that is automatically logged when you browse the Babybeanbags.com.au website. This information can include your IP address and the browser that you are using, however the information gathered is purely statistical and is solely used to further develop our site, systems and service. The information gathered in this manner is not linked to any personal information collected through membership or purchasing processes.

NewsletterOur website offers a regular email generated to advise members of new products and promotions on offer from Baby Beanbags. You can opt out of this email at any time.
