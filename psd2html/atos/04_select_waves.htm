There are two different types of waves: a Long Stay Wave and a Short Stay Wave.

The Long Stay Waves offer a 2 -3 night stay and the opportunity to bring a spouse or partner.

The Short Stay Waves have been designed for those who can’t be away for as long a period and is a one night stay and for one person only (no companions).

Please select three (3) dates you are able to attend. Please put them in order of preference and click the submit button. We will do our best to ensure we place you in your preferred date but please note this is based on availability. We encourage you to return your preference as soon as possible to avoid disappointment.

Atos is also a proud Worldwide IT Partner of the International Paralympic Committee. We will therefore be hosting a similar programme over the first few days of the Paralympic Games. The structure will be very similar to our Olympic programme and includes accommodation at the Landmark hotel, tickets to a Paralympic event(s), scheduled meals, transportation to the events and a visit to the Atos Technical Operation Centre (TOC).

If you’re interested in attending the Paralympic Games, please note, these dates need to be selected as one of your top 3 choices.
