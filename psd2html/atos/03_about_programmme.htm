The Programme

You will witness our IT performance "behind the scenes" and visit our team of business technologists at the main Technology Operational Centre located at the LONDON 2012 Headquarters. We have carefully chosen premier Olympic events for you to enjoy and watch our IT systems live. For each Olympic Games, we arrive three years in advance and provide our highly skilled people, the processes and the Information Technology systems to support the Games and ensure its success. We are offering you the opportunity to join us next July and August in London by attending our Olympic Programme which has been structured into 14 different "waves" or dates you can attend. Each "Wave" Includes:

  • A visit to the London 2012 Technical Operation Centre (TOC) and experience how Atos brings the Games to the World
  • Airport meet and greet and transfers (for designated airports)
  • Accommodation at the 5 Star Landmark Hotel
  • Ticket to a high profile Olympic event(s) to see the IT systems live
  • Transportation to scheduled events
  • All food and beverage
