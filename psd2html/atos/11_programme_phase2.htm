The Programme

You will witness our IT performance "behind the scenes" and visit our team of business technologists at the main Technology Operational Centre located at the LONDON 2012 Headquarters. We have carefully chosen premier Olympic events for you to enjoy and watch our IT systems live. For each Olympic Games, we arrive three years in advance and provide our highly skilled people, the processes and the Information Technology systems to support the Games and ensure its success.

Our 14 different "waves" have been structured into 3 different formats.

Long Stay Inclusions:

  • Visit to the Atos Technical Operation Centre (TOC) and experience how Atos brings the Games to the World
  • Depending on the wave a 2 -3 night stay at the 5 Star Landmark Hotel
  • Can bring a companion
  • Daily ticket to an Olympic Event
  • All food and beverage

Short Stay Inclusions:

  • Visit to the Atos Technical Operation Centre (TOC) and experience how Atos brings the Games to the World
  • 1 Night stay at the 5 Star Landmark Hotel
  • No companions allowed
  • Ticket to an Olympic Event
  • All food and beverage
  • All ground transfers

Paralympic Event Inclusions:

  • Visit to the Atos Technical Operation Centre (TOC) and experience how Atos brings the Games to the World
  • 2 night stay at the 5 Star Landmark Hotel
  • No companions allowed
  • Daily ticket to a Paralympic Event
  • All food and beverage
  • All ground transfers
