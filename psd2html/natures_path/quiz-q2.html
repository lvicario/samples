Question:

GMOs have the following effect:

  • A: GMO crops pose no threat to human health.
  • B: GMO crops yield more than organic crops.
  • C: There is no data. We just don’t know.

You're right

Actually...

There are no long term studies on the effects of GMO consumption to date. The Rodale Institute’s 30-Year Farming Systems Trial shows that organic crops build more soil fertility over time and can yield just as much as conventional systems – even outperforming GMO crops in times of drought and environmental changes.
