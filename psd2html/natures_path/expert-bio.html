“Hey Arran, what do you believe about Genetically Modified Organisms and how did you come to your understanding?” Facebook Twitter

Arran Stephens / arranstephens.com

Arran is a father, grandfather, husband, artist, entrepreneur, ecology advocate, and gardener who believes that everyone has a right to know what’s in their food. He co-foudned Nature’s Path Foods, a family-run, passionately independent, sustainably-driven organic food company. Arran is avidly involved with ongoing GMO labeling legislation. He’s facilitated Nature’s Path’s support of California’s Right to Know Campaign for Prop 37, supports 1522 in Washington, and sponsors the new GMO OMG documentary.
