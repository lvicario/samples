Question:

What percentage of packaged foods sold in the U.S. contain GMO ingredients?

You're right

Actually...

Eighty percent of packaged foods in the US contain GMO ingredients. Nine-four percent of US-grown soy, 90% of US-grown canola, and 88% of US-grown corn is genetically modified. These ingredients and their derivatives are found in millions of North American products. You might not know they are GMOs though, because there are no laws that require transparency on labels.
