Used Cars, Mt Gravatt

Our aim at Karland is to become a pillar stone in the local community as a trusted used car retailer so that Karland is the clear choice when deciding to purchase a used car and when choosing where to service your car. Our team have been carefully selected and are focused on accommodating our clients' needs to the best of their abilities. Between just four of our onsite managers Karland have more than 60 years experience in the motor trade. All our sales executives are fully trained and endeavour to provide a no pressure sales environment so that driving home in your next used car is easier than ever. At Karland you'll come to expect only the absolute best in used cars and first class customer service, all at competitive prices.
