Guaranteed income for retirement: All new. All different. All yours.

Aria's signature solutions for independent RIAs

As former senior executives at such industry-leading firms as Charles Schwab and Fidelity, Aria's executive team knew that independent RIAs were underserved and lacked advisor-facing tools for retirement income planning. So they founded Aria to give RIAs access to the customized solutions you need as your clients' needs shift from asset accumulation to retirement income protection and distribution.

Aria's open platform of low-cost and commission-free products gives you the power to provide your clients' retirement income a buffer from risks such as market volatility, longevity, inflation and adverse sequence of returns. With your choice of the highest-quality products from top insurers and industry-leading mutual funds and ETFs, you can serve each client with the strategy you deem best for their needs

Aria's market-changing RetireOne series of solutions provides a guaranteed income stream for a retiree's life that continues even when the investment account becomes depleted. The withdrawal benefit guarantee is provided on underlying assets that RIAs actively manage and it provides income protection when markets are down and income growth potential when markets are up.
