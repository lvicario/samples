Global Professional Services

It isn’t just about the software. It’s about the solution.

You've made the right choice by investing in a great software product! Our Global Professional Services (GPS) are here to help you maximize yourinvestment in our software. Our team blends knowledge, experience, tools, and a disciplined consulting approach and make sure you make the most of your software.

Our professional services team provides a complete line of consulting, technical, and educational offerings to help address your every need. Whether you are a brand new customer or a long-term client our solutions are designed to complement your requirements.

Not only are our solutions designed to fit your needs, but they are also designed to scale to the level needed to support your organization.

Solution Packages

Often used for new customer implementations. Our solution packages augment consulting with pre-configured solutions, techniques, and education to enable implementations to proceed quickly and at low cost.

Upgrades and Installation Offerings

Are you self-hosted or planning to implement MRI in your own data center? Are you planning to upgrade MRI to the latest release? Our Global Professional Services team has offerings designed to help you make your upgrade a smooth and easy process. Our upgrade offerings range from simple software-only-upgrades to comprehensive solutions that incorporate testing and training. Do you want to upgrade your existing server or move to a newer infrastructure? Global Professional Services can help you throughout the entire process from planning through to the migration to your new hardware.

GPS Factory

Our GPS team understands that costs can be an issue. One way we help control costs are by using our GPS Factory capabilities. With the GPS Factory we can do most of our consulting remotely out of our offices. This saves money and speeds delivery by eliminating the costs and time associated with travel.

Interfaces and Conversions

Global Professional Services can help you integrate other systems (internal or external) with MRI. Are you a new client or an existing client expanding your portfolio? Our Global Professional Services team has developed offerings to make data conversions as easy as possible.

Did you know that MRI can be integrated with other systems? It may be easier than you think! In fact, our Global Professional Services team has already created many of the most frequently requested interfaces.
