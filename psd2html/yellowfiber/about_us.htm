About Us

YellowFiber is a metro fiber optic company with a global presence. Providing on demand high bandwidth solutions since 2005; YellowFiber serves many of the Mid-Atlantic Fortune 1000 as well as medium sized firms. Yellow Fiber's Broadband Access changes everything - with a cost-effective, high-speed, "always-on" nationwide connectivity solution that is easy to deploy, manage and support

Our Internet access will allow you to communicate more effectively with your customers, partners, suppliers, employees and other stakeholders. We have the solution, whether you're an enterprise client with high-bandwidth requirements for connecting multiple branches, store fronts, teleworkers, or a small business with huge internet demands. YellowFiber delivers.

Our Facilities

All of our facililities are SAS70 type 2 certified and include:

  • 2N and N+1 AC UPS power, DC power is available
  • N+1 emergency generator configuration
  • N+1 cooling systems
  • Dual-interlock, dry-pipe pre-action fire suppression system with VESDA
  • 24×7 staffing and site access, CCTV surveillance, hardened data center perimeter and biometric access control
  • 24×7 remote hands and mechanical & electrical monitoring, including branch-circuit monitoring from certified data center technicians
  • Carrier-neutral access to over 50 networks and service providers through diverse points of entry Ample space for antenna towers with line-of-sight in all directions
