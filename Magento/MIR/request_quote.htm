The Commercial Division of Materials In The Raw is committed to delivering quality products at competitively low prices, utilising our industry experience & supplier relationships. We appreciate how important time is to our customer’s and to that end we have 4 well located stores throughout Sydney that stock over a $1 million of inventory providing fast service, product availability & superior delivery scheduling.

Materials in the Raw understands that no job may be the same. Specialty quoting is available offering project prices on store products either picked up or delivered & we will provide contract pricing on those large jobs via our Commercial Division.

Our product range is extensive, providing all facets of raw material and hardware for the Developer, Builder and Landscaper.
